## 6. FAQ Section
- **Headline:** Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle:** Addressing common concerns before we start.
- **Q&A Items:**
  - **Q:** How long does it take to see results?
    **A:** Google Ads can start generating traffic almost immediately after launch. However, meaningful and optimized results typically appear within 2 to 4 weeks as we gather data and refine the campaigns.
  - **Q:** How much budget do I need to start?
    **A:** The ideal budget depends on your industry and competition. Most clients start from RM 30 to RM 100/day, but we’ll recommend a strategy based on your goals and market demand.
  - **Q:** Do you guarantee results?
    **A:** We don’t believe in unrealistic guarantees. What we do guarantee is a data driven strategy, continuous optimization, and full transparency to maximize your ROI.
  - **Q:** What’s included in your Google Ads management service?
    **A:** Our service includes: Campaign setup and structure, Keyword research (high intent focus), Ad copywriting, Conversion tracking setup, Ongoing optimization and scaling.
  - **Q:** Will I have access to my Google Ads account?
    **A:** Yes. You will have full ownership and access to your account at all times. We operate with complete transparency.
  - **Q:** How do you ensure my ads attract quality buyers?
    **A:** We focus on high intent keywords, precise targeting, and optimized ad messaging to reach users who are actively searching and ready to take action.
  - **Q:** Do you handle landing pages as well?
    **A:** Yes. We can design or optimize high converting landing pages to ensure your ad traffic turns into actual leads or sales.
  - **Q:** What if my ads are not performing well?
    **A:** We continuously monitor performance and make data driven adjustments, including keywords, bidding, targeting, and creatives, to improve results.
  - **Q:** Can I stop or pause anytime?
    **A:** Yes. There are no long term lock ins. You have full control to pause, scale, or stop campaigns anytime.
  - **Q:** How do you measure success?
    **A:** We track key metrics such as: Cost per lead (CPL), Conversion rate, Return on ad spend (ROAS), Revenue generated. Everything is clearly reported so you know exactly where your money goes.
