## 6. FAQ Section
- **Headline:** Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle:** Addressing common concerns before we start.
- **Q&A Items:**
  - **Q:** How long does it take to see results?
    **A:** Meta Ads can generate traffic quickly, often within 24 to 72 hours after launch. However, optimal performance usually takes 2 to 3 weeks as we test creatives, audiences, and optimize campaigns.
  - **Q:** How much budget do I need to start?
    **A:** We typically recommend starting from RM 30 to RM 150 per day, depending on your niche and goals. A proper testing budget is important to identify winning creatives and audiences.
  - **Q:** Do you create the ad creatives as well?
    **A:** Yes. We help with creative direction, messaging, and ad structure. If needed, we can also assist with content ideas tailored for high engagement and conversions.
  - **Q:** What makes Meta Ads different from Google Ads?
    **A:** Meta Ads focus on capturing attention and creating demand, while Google Ads target users who are already searching. With the right strategy, Meta Ads can scale your brand faster.
  - **Q:** Do you guarantee sales?
    **A:** No agency can guarantee sales, but we focus on what matters: 👉 data driven testing, strong creatives, and continuous optimization to maximize your return.
  - **Q:** How do you ensure my ads perform well?
    **A:** We focus on: Scroll stopping creatives, Clear messaging and hooks, Audience testing (broad + targeted), Continuous A/B testing. Everything is optimized based on real performance data.
  - **Q:** What if my ads are not performing?
    **A:** We continuously monitor campaigns and adjust: Creatives, Targeting, Budget allocation. If something doesn’t work, we pivot fast to find what does.
  - **Q:** Will I have access to my ad account?
    **A:** Yes. You will have full access and ownership of your Meta Ads account at all times.
  - **Q:** Do you handle landing pages as well?
    **A:** Yes. We can design or optimize high converting landing pages to ensure your ad traffic turns into actual leads or sales.
  - **Q:** Can I scale my ads once they are profitable?
    **A:** Yes. Once we identify winning campaigns, we scale using proven strategies while maintaining performance and cost efficiency.
  - **Q:** Is this suitable for new businesses?
    **A:** Yes. Meta Ads are ideal for businesses that want to: Generate awareness quickly, Test product market fit, Start getting sales even without organic traffic.
