## 5. FAQ Section
- **Headline:** Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle:** Addressing common concerns before we start.
- **Q&A Items:**
  - **Q:** How long does it take?
    **A:** Most projects are completed within 3 to 7 working days, depending on scope and content readiness. Urgent projects can be prioritized upon request.
  - **Q:** Do you provide content (copywriting & images)?
    **A:** Yes. We can handle basic copywriting, layout structure, and visual direction. However, for best results, we recommend providing your product details, brand assets, and key messaging.
  - **Q:** Will my website be optimized for conversions?
    **A:** Absolutely. Every page is designed with a conversion first approach, including strategic layout, call to actions, and user flow to maximize leads and sales.
  - **Q:** Is SEO included?
    **A:** Yes, we implement basic on page SEO such as proper structure, meta tags, and performance optimization. Advanced SEO services can be added separately.
  - **Q:** Can I request revisions?
    **A:** Yes. We provide revision rounds to ensure the final design meets your expectations and business goals.
  - **Q:** Will my website be mobile friendly?
    **A:** 100%. Your website will be fully responsive, optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
  - **Q:** Can I upgrade or scale my website later?
    **A:** Yes. Your website is built with scalability in mind, allowing future upgrades such as additional pages, features, or integrations.
  - **Q:** Do you provide domain and hosting?
    **A:** We can guide you through the setup or manage it for you. Domain and hosting are charged separately unless included in your package.
  - **Q:** What platform do you use?
    **A:** We primarily build using WordPress + Elementor, ensuring flexibility, ease of management, and long term scalability.
  - **Q:** Will I be able to edit the website myself?
    **A:** Yes. You’ll have full access and can easily update content using a user friendly editor without technical skills.
  - **Q:** Do you integrate payment systems or forms?
    **A:** Yes. We can integrate payment gateways, lead forms, WhatsApp buttons, and automation tools based on your needs.
  - **Q:** What if I don’t have any technical knowledge?
    **A:** No problem. We handle everything from setup to launch. You just focus on your business, we take care of the rest.
